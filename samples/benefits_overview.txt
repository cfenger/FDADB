Company Benefits Overview
-------------------------

Health:
- Medical and dental coverage start on day one.
- Telemedicine visits are free for employees and dependents.

Time off:
- 15 days of PTO per year, accrued monthly.
- Company-wide recharge day on the first Friday of each quarter.

Professional growth:
- Annual learning stipend of $1,500 for courses or certifications.
- Internal mentorship program pairing new hires with senior staff.
